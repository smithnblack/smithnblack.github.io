IBD Benchmarking Portal

Benchmarking Industry is growing rapidly in the past decade. To be able to follow this trend, we need something that is powerful to follow and distinct those benchmarks without losing all the important data from the results that have been made. Daily need of benchmark results in companies can be difficult if there is no adequate tool where the benchmarks are being compared. Not every information from those benchmarks are relevant to all users. There are different kind of web portals that serve those needs of different kind of users, but just few of them are able to show us fast calculations and comparison with nice graphics between benchmarks that every user is able to understand and interpret. TPC and SPEC are benchmarking portals that are missing interactive functionalities. Poor navigation possibility needs to be improved and graphical presentation is missing. The main goal was to create a benchmarking portal that is user friendly, easy to understand and intuitive and implements missing features of other portals available online.

Our goal is to present and sort all kind of benchmarks and let user be able to check, compare and be up to date.
